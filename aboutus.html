History

Founded in 2007, Arminas Travel is a premier Destination Management Company (DMC) based in Mexico. Since its inception, the company has catered to over 10,000 travelers annually, offering specialized services for individual adventurers, group travelers, and corporate clients focused on meetings and incentives. Arminas Travel is dedicated to delivering high-quality and reliable services, ensuring maximum satisfaction for clients from regions such as Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Services

Arminas Travel offers an extensive range of private services across the Mexican Republic, thoughtfully organized and coordinated by our team of highly experienced professionals. We provide accommodations in the most luxurious and stunning hotels, along with captivating excursions and comprehensive tours throughout the country. Our transportation services feature modern, comfortable vehicles, and our expert tour guides ensure a rich and engaging journey.

With over 100 well-curated and operated tours in Mexico, we create exceptional itineraries tailored to the unique requirements of each client. At Arminas Travel, we pride ourselves on delivering bespoke services that perfectly align with our customers' needs, making every journey unforgettable.

Our services are available in English, Spanish, and any other languages you may require, with 24-hour support to ensure a seamless experience

Highlights & Awards

  • In 2010, Mrs. Armina Wolpert was personally invited by the President of the Republic to participate in the “Launch of the Tourist Routes of Mexico” on behalf of Arminas Travel, which is regarded as one of the premier operators in the country.

Our CEO, Armina Wolpert in event of 'Routes of Mexico' with Former President of Mexico, Mr. Felipe Calderon and Foreign Affairs Secretary Mrs. Patricia Espinoza. 2010

  • At the Tianguis Turístico de México 2022 in Acapulco, our esteemed Director and Founder, Mrs. Armina Wolpert, was honored with a distinguished award for her exceptional contributions to promoting tourism from Europe to Mexico. This prestigious accolade was presented to her by the Governor of Guerrero, alongside other eminent federal authorities.

  • Arminas Travel has consistently been recognized and awarded by the Secretary of Tourism of Mexico. We take great pride in officially representing Los Cabos and Mayakoba as premier tourist destinations in Eastern Europe.

Highest level of recognition of Mexican tourism industry for our company awarded by Minister of Interior Adán Augusto López Hernández and Minister of Tourism, Miguel Torruco
