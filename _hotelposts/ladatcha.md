### Introduction  

Perched directly on the dramatic Pacific coastline of Cabo San Lucas, La Datcha is an extraordinary private villa where bold design, exceptional service, and complete exclusivity come together in spectacular fashion. Inspired by the traditional Mexican hacienda yet infused with eclectic global influences, this ultra-luxury retreat offers a one-of-a-kind experience designed for unforgettable celebrations, multigenerational escapes, and private gatherings in one of Mexico’s most coveted coastal destinations. 

![](/images/hotels/ladatcha/ladatcha1.jpg)

### Location  

Located within the prestigious gated community of Pedregal, La Datcha enjoys a privileged beachfront setting overlooking the Pacific Ocean. Despite its secluded atmosphere, the villa is only minutes from Cabo San Lucas marina, world-class dining, luxury boutiques, and vibrant nightlife. The surrounding Baja landscape creates a striking backdrop where desert cliffs meet the sea. 

![](/images/hotels/ladatcha/ladatcha3.jpg)

### What to Do  

Days at La Datcha can be as relaxing or as adventure-filled as desired. Guests can spend time beside the expansive infinity pool, unwind in the spa featuring sauna, Turkish bath, and Mexico’s first snow room, or enjoy personalised wellness sessions with private trainers and spa therapists. Beyond the villa, Cabo offers world-renowned sportfishing, whale watching from November to April, yacht excursions, ATV adventures, surfing, tequila tastings, and private culinary experiences curated by the villa’s team. 

![](/images/hotels/ladatcha/ladatcha4.jpg)

### Why We Recommend This Hotel  

A truly exclusive beachfront estate — Located directly on the sand within Pedregal, offering privacy, security, and uninterrupted Pacific Ocean views. &nbsp;  

Remarkable design with global influences — Mexican hacienda architecture is elevated through colourful textiles, handcrafted décor, mosaics, and sophisticated international touches. &nbsp;  

Exceptional personalised service — A dedicated team including chefs, butlers, drivers, wellness staff, and concierge creates a fully tailored experience. &nbsp;  

An extraordinary wellness experience — The spa facilities include a sauna, steam room, Turkish bath, massage rooms, and a unique snow room rarely found anywhere in the world. &nbsp;  

Perfect for celebrations and group escapes — With 10 bedrooms accommodating up to 20 guests, La Datcha is ideal for milestone events, family gatherings, and luxurious retreats. &nbsp;  

![](/images/hotels/ladatcha/ladatcha5.jpg)

### Dining Options  

Dining at La Datcha is entirely personalised, with world-class chefs preparing everything from authentic Mexican cuisine to international specialities tailored to guests’ preferences. Multiple indoor and outdoor dining spaces allow for intimate breakfasts overlooking the ocean, elegant dinners beside the fire-lit pool, or lively gatherings with cocktails and live entertainment. The villa also houses an impressive private wine collection carefully curated over many years.
