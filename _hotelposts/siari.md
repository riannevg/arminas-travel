## Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve, Riviera Nayarit &nbsp;

### Introduction  
Perched between the emerald Sierra Madre mountains and the vast Pacific Ocean, **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** is a secluded sanctuary where timeless nature meets refined luxury. This exclusive retreat honors the land, weaving together lush jungle, mangrove estuaries, and golden-sand coastline in a tranquil tapestry that uplifts the spirit and slows the pace of time.  

![](/images/hotels/siari/siari2.jpeg)


### Location  
Located within the private, master-planned community of Nauka, just north of Puerto Vallarta, **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** spans over **920 acres** and stretches along nearly **4.3 miles** of untouched beach. Its remote setting offers guests a deep sense of privacy while still being accessible, approximately **70 km from Puerto Vallarta International Airport**.  

![](/images/hotels/siari/siari3.jpeg)

### What to Do  
At **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve**, exploration is guided by nature and tradition. Wander through the jungle to discover hidden waterfalls, or find stillness in a secluded cove perfect for meditation. Venture out on the ocean for **whale-watching** during the season, or book a tour to learn about the region’s **Huichol culture**, its rituals, and craft traditions. Guests also have access to exclusive amenities, including the **Nauka Golf Club**, an 18-hole championship course designed by Tom Fazio.  

![](/images/hotels/siari/siari4.jpeg)

### Why We Recommend This Hotel  
A sanctuary of serenity – **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** feels like a hidden world, where nature is honored and preserved in every corner. &nbsp;  
&nbsp;  
Authentic cultural immersion – The heart of the resort draws on Huichol traditions, from its wellness rituals to its architecture and design, offering an experience deeply rooted in local heritage. &nbsp;  
&nbsp;  
Wild yet refined – With expansive protected grounds, low-density architecture, and ocean-facing suites, **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** gifts guests both privacy and connection. &nbsp;  
&nbsp;  
Holistic wellness – The **Há Yeka Wellness Center** uses jungle botanicals, volcanic stones, and indigenous-inspired rituals to nurture body and mind. &nbsp;  
&nbsp;  
Tailored luxury – From cliffside villas to open-air suites, every space is designed to frame the natural beauty of the Riviera Nayarit, with interiors that blend elegance and local artistry. &nbsp;  

![](/images/hotels/siari/siari5.jpeg)

### Dining Options  
At **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve**, gastronomy is a journey into the soul of Riviera Nayarit. The resort’s culinary experiences emphasize **locally sourced ingredients**, sustainable seafood, and deep-rooted traditions. The signature **Zula by David Castro Hussong**, overlooking the Pacific, draws inspiration from both land and sea, serving dishes that celebrate generational flavours with a modern twist. Whether enjoying fresh coastal seafood or dishes infused with indigenous culinary heritage, dining at **Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** is an encounter with place, culture, and taste.  

![](/images/hotels/siari/siari6.jpeg)

### Accommodations  
**Siari, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** offers a range of suites and villas designed to blur the boundaries between indoors and out. Some overlook the golden beach, while others rise against lush, black-rock cliffs, each featuring wide terraces, plunge pools, and panoramic views of the surrounding nature.
