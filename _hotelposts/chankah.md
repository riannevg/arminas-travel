### Introduction  

Immersed in the heart of the Chiapas jungle, Chan-Kah Resort Village is a unique eco-retreat where nature takes centre stage. Meaning “small village” in Mayan, the resort lives up to its name by offering a tranquil, village-like atmosphere surrounded by lush vegetation, flowing rivers, and abundant wildlife. Designed to blend harmoniously with its environment, it provides an authentic and peaceful escape just moments from one of Mexico’s most remarkable archaeological sites.  

![](/images/hotels/chankah/chankah3.jpg)

### Location  

Located just minutes from the Palenque archaeological zone, Chan-Kah enjoys a privileged setting along the road to the ruins, surrounded by over 50 acres of tropical jungle, gardens, and natural waterways. This strategic yet secluded location makes it an ideal base for exploring both the cultural treasures and natural wonders of the region.  

![](/images/hotels/chankah/chankah5.webp)

### What to Do  

Discover the ancient Mayan city of Palenque, hidden within the jungle, or venture further to explore waterfalls such as Agua Azul and Misol-Ha. Within the resort itself, guests can unwind by lagoon-style pools inspired by natural cenotes, stroll through jungle paths, or simply listen to the sounds of the surrounding wildlife. Wellness experiences, including traditional temazcal rituals and spa treatments, offer a deeper connection to the region’s ancestral traditions.  

![](/images/hotels/chankah/chankah7.jpg)

### Why We Recommend This Hotel  

An immersive jungle setting — Surrounded by rivers, tropical vegetation, and wildlife, the hotel offers a true escape into nature. &nbsp;  

Unique lagoon-style pools — Designed to resemble natural cenotes, the pools create a distinctive and memorable atmosphere. &nbsp;  

Authentic bungalow-style accommodation — Private casitas with terraces allow guests to fully enjoy the sights and sounds of the jungle. &nbsp;  

Proximity to Palenque ruins — Just a short drive from one of the most important Mayan archaeological sites. &nbsp;  

Wellness inspired by ancient traditions — The Maya Spa and temazcal experiences provide a meaningful and restorative touch. &nbsp;  

![](/images/hotels/chankah/chankah8.jpg)

### Dining Options  

Dining at Chan-Kah offers a relaxed and authentic experience, with restaurants and terraces overlooking the pools and jungle surroundings. Guests can enjoy a variety of regional and Mexican dishes, often accompanied by the natural soundtrack of the forest. A cafeteria and bar provide additional options for casual meals and refreshing drinks throughout the day.
