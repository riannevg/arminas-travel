## Rosewood Mandarina &nbsp;

Set between lush rainforest and the vast Pacific Ocean, Rosewood Mandarina is a sanctuary where untamed nature and refined luxury exist in perfect harmony. Designed to immerse guests in the spirit of Riviera Nayarit, this extraordinary resort invites a deep connection with the land, blending contemporary elegance with indigenous heritage and the raw beauty of Mexico’s coastline.  

![](/images/hotels/rosewoodman/rosewoodman.png)

### Location  

Located along the spectacular Riviera Nayarit coastline, Rosewood Mandarina sits within a pristine natural enclave of jungle, mountains, and secluded beaches. The resort is surrounded by dramatic landscapes shaped by the Sierra Madre mountains, offering a sense of complete seclusion while still within reach of Puerto Vallarta and Punta Mita.  

![](/images/hotels/rosewoodman/rosewoodman2.png)

### What to Do  

Days unfold through curated experiences that celebrate both adventure and wellbeing. Guests can explore ancient trails on guided jungle hikes, discover hidden beaches, or take a boat trip to the protected Islas Marietas. For a slower pace, the Asaya Spa offers holistic rituals inspired by local traditions, while the Polo & Equestrian Club invites a unique connection with Mandarina’s majestic horses. Every experience is designed to reconnect you with nature in its purest form.  

![](/images/hotels/rosewoodman/rosewoodman4.png)

### Why We Recommend This Hotel  

**A seamless connection to nature** — The resort is thoughtfully integrated into jungle, mountain, and ocean landscapes, creating a truly immersive environment. &nbsp;  

**All-suite privacy with elevated design** — Each accommodation features expansive indoor and outdoor living spaces, many with private plunge pools and panoramic views. &nbsp;  

**Exceptional culinary experiences** — From beachfront dining to cliffside mixology and Japanese-Mexican fusion cuisine, every venue is distinctive and memorable. &nbsp;  

**Wellness rooted in local traditions** — The Asaya Spa and curated rituals offer a meaningful and restorative approach to wellbeing. &nbsp;  

**A destination within a destination** — With its diverse landscapes and experiences, the resort offers endless discovery without ever needing to leave. &nbsp;  

![](/images/hotels/rosewoodman/rosewoodman5.jpg)

### Dining Options  

Dining at Rosewood Mandarina is a celebration of both place and creativity. La Cocina Mandarina showcases traditional Mexican techniques, including handmade tortillas prepared on a comal, while Buena Onda offers a relaxed beachfront atmosphere with seafood and Mediterranean influences. For something truly unique, Toppu presents elevated Japanese cuisine infused with Mexican flavours, and Barra Peñasco delivers an unforgettable cocktail experience set dramatically above the ocean.
