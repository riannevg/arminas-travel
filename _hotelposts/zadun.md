### Introduction  
Where the desert meets the Sea of Cortés, **Zadún, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** is one of Mexico’s most extraordinary luxury retreats. Nestled within the exclusive enclave of Puerto Los Cabos, this intimate sanctuary captures the essence of Baja California Sur through thoughtful design, personalised service, and a profound connection to nature. Every moment here is crafted to celebrate the surrounding landscape, from the golden dunes and dramatic coastline to the rich cultural traditions that inspire the resort’s architecture, gastronomy, and wellness experiences.  

![](/images/hotels/zadun/zadun7.jpg)

### Location  
Located in the prestigious community of Puerto Los Cabos, **Zadún, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** enjoys a privileged setting overlooking the Sea of Cortés, just minutes from the charming town of San José del Cabo and approximately five kilometres from Los Cabos International Airport. Despite its convenient location, the resort feels wonderfully secluded, offering guests a sense of privacy and tranquillity surrounded by desert landscapes, rolling dunes, and endless ocean views. 

![](/images/hotels/zadun/zadun6.jpg)

### What to Do  
Days at **Zadún, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** can be as active or relaxed as you wish. Indulge in transformative wellness programmes through the renowned Sensei partnership, with personalised consultations focused on movement, nutrition, mindfulness, and sleep. Explore the Sea of Cortés with guided marine experiences, discover local wildlife alongside expert naturalists through the Ambassadors of the Environment programme, or spend the afternoon on the acclaimed Puerto Los Cabos Golf Club, designed by Greg Norman and Jack Nicklaus. For those seeking cultural immersion, San José del Cabo's galleries, boutiques, and historic streets are just a short drive away. 

![](/images/hotels/zadun/zadun2.jpg)

### Dining Options  
Dining at **Zadún, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** is a celebration of Mexican culinary heritage, reimagined through contemporary techniques and exceptional local ingredients. The resort's restaurants draw inspiration from the traditions of Los Cabos and the wider regions of Mexico, creating memorable dining experiences that honour both land and sea. From vibrant market-inspired cuisine and freshly caught seafood to expertly grilled specialties and curated agave tastings, every meal reflects the flavours, craftsmanship, and cultural richness of Baja California Sur. Guests can also enjoy an impressive selection of Mexican wines, artisanal cocktails, and intimate dining experiences overlooking the Sea of Cortés.
