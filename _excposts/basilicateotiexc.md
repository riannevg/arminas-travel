## Teotihuacan and Basilica de Guadalupe 
&nbsp;  

### Main Information

Begin your adventure with an immersive Mexico City sightseeing tour, where you'll explore the iconic landmarks of the Mexican capital. Discover the vibrant ZÃ³calo main square, the ancient ruins of the Aztec empire Tenochtitlan, the majestic Mexico City Cathedral, and the historic National Palace. As you travel along the bustling avenue Paseo de la Reforma, you'll experience the dynamic heart of Mexico City, surrounded by elegant architecture, monuments, and the lively rhythm of the city.  

From there, continue your journey to one of the most extraordinary sites of pre-Columbian history: the Teotihuacan archaeological complex. Located just an hour outside Mexico City, Teotihuacan allows you to step back in time and explore the grandeur of this ancient metropolis, whose influence shaped the Aztec world centuries later. Ascend the Pyramids of the Sun and the Moon, wander along the Avenue of the Dead, and visit the Temple of Quetzalcoatl and the Butterflies Temple, all while marveling at the scale, symmetry, and spiritual power of this remarkable civilization. For a truly unforgettable experience, you may also choose to take a hot-air balloon ride at sunrise, soaring above the pyramids and gaining a breathtaking perspective of the city below.

### Teotihuacan Archaeological Site

Teotihuacan stands as one of the most impressive remnants of ancient Mesoamerica. Its vast layout, monumental pyramids, and intricate temples reveal the complexity of its society, religion, and art. Walking through this archaeological wonder, you will feel the grandeur and mystique that have drawn visitors for centuries, while gaining insight into the spiritual and cultural legacy of the region.  

![Teotihuacan](/images/destinations/cdmx/teotihuacan3.jpg)

### The Shrine of Our Lady of Guadalupe

A journey to one of the most significant pilgrimage sites in Catholicism, the Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe, awaits you. Visited by several million people each year, this sacred site holds deep spiritual significance. Follow in the footsteps of pilgrims as you climb Mount Tepeyac and explore six remarkable churches, experiencing both the historical and devotional importance of this emblematic location.
